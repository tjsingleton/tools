### Reprocess flag
`--reprocess` on `kp run` skips ingest+normalize and yields raws from the
latest `ItemNormalized` event per content_hash. Implementation:
`reprocess_stage()` in `src/kp/pipeline/stages/ingest.py`, plumbed through
`PipelineRunner.run(reprocess=True)` and the CLI `run` command.

### WhisperX 3.8 API notes (in case of future bumps)
- `DiarizationPipeline` lives in `whisperx.diarize`, not the top-level module.
- Constructor kwarg is `token=` (not `use_auth_token=`).
- Default model in 3.8 is `pyannote/speaker-diarization-community-1`
  (separate gating from `speaker-diarization-3.1`).
